Our Mission

At Loop Optometry, we are committed to providing our best to our patients and to the San Francisco community. Your vision is our top priority and our goal is to exceed your expectations with our level of care. We strive to simplify your eye care experience by guiding you through every step, from your eye examination to selecting the ideal frame/lens/contact lenses to assisting you in understanding your insurance coverage. We invite you to ask any questions and to learn more about our team by stopping by any time.
