The Loop Team

Dr. May Szeto, OD, has over eight years of unique experience working in various private ophthalmology and retail optometry practices in New York City and in the Bay Area. After completion of her Doctorate of Optometry at SUNY College of Optometry, Dr. Szeto worked in New York City's high end optical shops as well as Gouverneur Hospital, which provides affordable health care to the Lower East Side population. Upon moving back to San Francisco, she served as the optometrist and office manager at the Glaucoma Center of San Francisco and a research coordinator for the Glaucoma Research and Education Group.

Dr Szeto founded Loop Optometry in 2015 and is proud to provide a combination of excellent eye care, personal care, and style to your experience. Come by any time to say hello and you might just meet the Loopmeister.
