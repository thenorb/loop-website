COVID19 Update

In light of the evolving pandemic and shelter in place order made by the mayor, we are closed until April 7th. Our top priority is the health and well-being of our patients, families, staff and community.

If you ordered glasses or contact lenses before our closure and have been notified they are ready for pick up, please email us at frontdesk@looptometry.com to coordinate a time to pick them up from our office. If you haven't heard from us, don't worry, we are processing all existing orders and we will notify you ASAP. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions/concerns. We are unable to troubleshoot or adjust glasses at this time, but will be more than happy to do so when we reopen.

We have now included an online ordering form for contact lenses. We are offering FREE shipping with a minimum order of 2 boxes. Please email us at frontdesk@looptometry.com to place your order or fill out the new form on our website.

The Loop Team

Dr. May Szeto, OD, has over ten years of unique experience working in various private ophthalmology and retail optometry practices in New York City and in the Bay Area. After completion of her Doctorate of Optometry at SUNY College of Optometry, Dr. Szeto worked in New York City's high end optical shops as well as Gouverneur Hospital, which provides affordable health care to the Lower East Side population. Upon moving back to San Francisco, she served as the optometrist and office manager at the Glaucoma Center of San Francisco and a research coordinator for the Glaucoma Research and Education Group.

Dr. Szeto founded Loop Optometry in 2015 and is proud to provide a combination of excellent eye care, personal care, and style to your experience. Come by any time to say hello and you might just meet the Loopmeister.
